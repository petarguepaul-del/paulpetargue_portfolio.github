what is chicken bopis?

Bopis is a Filipino dish made from pork or beef lungs and heart, finely chopped and sautéed with onions, garlic, vinegar, and chili peppers. It’s known for its bold, spicy, tangy flavor and crisp texture.

Ingredients

  • 1lb pork lungs, cleaned
  • ½ lb pork heart
  • 6 cups water (boiling)
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 5 gloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • ¼ cup vinegar
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp annatto powder
  • 3-5 pcs red chilli peppers
  • salt
  • 1 small carot
  • 1 redor green bell peppers
  • a pintch of a sugar

Instruction

  • in a pot, place the pork lungs and heat with enough water tp cover.
  • boil for 30-45 minutes or until tender.
  • remove from a pot, let cool, and chop finely into a small cubes.
  • heat cooking oil in the pan or wok over medium heat.
  • saute garlic, thean add onion and tomatoes until soft and fragrant.
  • add the chopped pork lungs and heart.
  • stir-fry for 5-10 minutes until slightly browned.
  • add soy sauce, vinegar, and bay leaves.
  • let it simmer without stirring for about 2 minutes (to let the vinegar cook.").
  • then stir and add annatto powder, chilli, and black pepper.
  • lower heat and cook for 10-15 minutes, allowing flavors to blend.
  • if using carrots or bell peppers, add them at this stage.
  • add salt and a pinch of sugar if desired.
  • continue cooking until the mixture is dry or saucy to your liking.
